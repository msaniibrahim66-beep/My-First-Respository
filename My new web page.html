Grubs And Foods Agro Tech Ltd Tracebility Portal

About

Grubs and Foods Agro Tech Business is an agricultural exporting company based in Agidingbi, Nigeria. They specialize in sourcing and exporting high quality agricultural products like Cassia Tora seeds, seasame seeds, and dried orange peels.This portal serves as the centralized digital interface for managing tracebility data compliance, activities, reports, dashboards, and integration with external systems as export platforms and laboratory data services related to GFAT [Grubs and Foods Agro Tech Ltd]
